To Oly, My Favourite Companion

To Oly, who brought beautiful memories and warmth into my life...

You were always a part of my days, your warmth will always be felt in my memories.

"You may have been only a part of my life but to you, I was your whole life."

Honestly, after the day you were gone, I still cannot consider another cat like you.

I can't even adopt other cats because I'm afraid I will be heartbroken again when the cat is gone, just like I was when I lost you.

I still remember my memories with you

  • Playing together in the park
  • When you kept me company during sad times
  • The way you ran to me as you saw my car appear at the car porch

I hope I can find a cat as good as you even though I know no one can replace you, Oly.


With all my love,
Nora
